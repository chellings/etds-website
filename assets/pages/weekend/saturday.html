Where to go on Saturday

It is Saturday and you want to go out and eat or drink something in Delft. Ideally by now you have reserved, but if you haven't, here are some tips for restaurants, take-outs, etc. Don't forget to be back in time for the prize-presentations and the show!

Suggested restaurants in Delft

  • Café de Wijnhaven
  • Belvedere
  • The hangout (on campus)
  • De Waag
  • Ciao Ciao
  • Fratelli
  • La Cubanita
  • De Beierd
  • De Ark van Delft
  • Huszár

For every single one of them the advice is to reserve ASAP, especially for large groups.

How to get to Delft center

After a 12 minute walk, at the Aula, you can take the bus (2 min) to "Delft Zuidpoort". Want to walk instead? That is 23 minutes.

From there, all aforementioned restaurants are within a 10 minute walk.

You can also park at:
  • Zuidpoort Garage
  • Phoenix Garage
  • Market Garage

Important: Do not enter the center of Delft by car, else you may be fined!

Dine at the venue

Having food at the venue is possible but only for those that have reserved before.

Those that have reserved can pick up their food at the X Café at 20:00.

Ordering food

During the day and in the evening, ordering food is not allowed at the venue. However, feel free to order anything anywhere else on campus.

Supermarket

There is a supermarket on campus. The SPAR, open in the weekend from 10AM - 10PM.

Drive-thru

Want the proper after-competition experience? Grab some fast food by car.
  • McDonald's (open 24/7)
  • Burger King
  • KFC
