Changes as opposed to other ETDSses

The floors are not numbered A and B but 0 and 1. Here, floor 0 is farthest from the tournament desk and stage. In other words, viewed from the team tables, it is the left floor.

There is a beginners class that requires a count in.

The open class has a qualification round that then splits into two classes. No big deal. However, after this they go by the names:
  • Rising Ravers - Or Ravers for short - Which replace the Closed class (rising is to indicate the lower level)
  • Risen Robots - Or Robots for short - Which replace the Open class (risen is to indicate the higher level)
Please use these names at all times (later on in the weekend you can switch to the shorter names)

The open class qualification as well as the ravers and robots have changing dances up to and including the quarter-finals. These will be simply pulled from a hat and will be danced in the order they are drawn in. The drawing should be done when the first heat of the dance is on the floor. However, the second dance will only be announced after all heats of the first dance have taken place. The same goes for the third dance.

The sleeping halls will be closed from 11-16

The prize presentation will be in the evening

The ravers and robots finals will both have 6 dances (which 6th dance will be announced after the semi final) and exactly 6 couples

The ravers and robots will have a lottery starting in their semi-finals. For the semi-finals, the order will be announced in advance through the portal. For the final, the order will not be announced in advance. Instead, please ask everyone on the floor and ask them to line up in the order we give you. You will simply tell them who will be dancing with whom. For the next dance, they will likely simply have to shift over one partner. But we will tell you on the weekend.

The team presentations on saturday are more similar to the NTDS-style team presentations. They have a total of 15 seconds combined for the walk-on and walk-off. In between they have a maximum of 30s of music. The music may slightly run into the walk-off, but the show may not last longer than 30s. Please make sure you or someone from the tournament desk times this. If they go over 30s the music should be cut. If no music was provided, simply announce the team normal ETDS style. We will provide you with a list and order for this.

Blind dating

Your main task on Friday is managing the blind dating.

At 20:30, the orga will do the official welcome, after which we will introduce you, and you will take over. Immediately after the blind dating starts.

The order of blind date rounds is as shown below. For more information, see the weekend planning under the button above.
  • Blind date round 1 STD breitensport
  • Blind date round 1 STD open + beginners (split floor)
  • Blind date round 1 LAT breitensport
  • Blind date round 1 LAT open + beginners (split floor)
  • Free dancing
  • Blind date round 2 STD breitensport
  • Blind date round 2 STD open + beginners (split floor)
  • Blind date round 2 LAT breitensport
  • Blind date round 2 LAT open + beginners (split floor)
  • Free dancing
  • Blind date round 3 LAT breitensport + beginners (split floor)
  • Blind date round 3 LAT open
  • Free dancing
  • Finalize blind date (split floor)

The way a blind date round is structured:
  • In rounds 1 and 2 of both standard and latin, there will be 3 dances each and then again 3 dances each.
  • In other words, for standard, it's SW TG QS SW TG QS.
  • In every song, after every 30s of dancing ask the dancers to find another partner.
  • For every dance there are 4 short sections of dancing.
  • This means 4x(30+7.5)s = 2m30s for each dance.
  • In total this leads to 15 minutes per round.

  • In rounds 3 of standard and latin, there will only 3 dances but you will play 5 sections of music of each 30s, totalling a round of about 10 minutes.

During the finalizing round, it is your job to find partners for all remaining blind daters. So please find team captains willing to help for this. Nevertheless, blind date rules may never be broken! For the open class, you may ask the highest ranking breitensport class finalist to help in the open. Please check this with Remy.

On saturday there will be one more round of blind dating for each, after which the finalizing is done again.
