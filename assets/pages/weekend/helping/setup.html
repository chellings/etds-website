Setup

Where do I report?

At the beginning of your shift you go to the tournament hall and look for the manager setup. Most likely, he will be walking around in a navy shirt and coordinating the process. He will be the one to give you a task.

What are my tasks?

In general you will be following the instructions given to you by the manager setup, this may include helping to build or remove the dance floor, under-floor, team tables, info desk, tournament desk, decorations, etc.

What are important things to keep in mind?

The equipment for lights and sound will be handled by technicians from the supplier, please do not touch this equipment unless specifically asked.
