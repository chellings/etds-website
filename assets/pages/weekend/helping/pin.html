Using the pin machine

How to make a payment

  • Unlock the tablet and open the SumUp app, if not already opened
  • Select the items the payment is for
  • (If desired, you can filter by Bar or Reception)
  • If applicable, choose the variant of the item
  • Press the charge button
  • Choose "Card Reader" and wait until the card reader connects
  • Let the person pay and possibly let them enter their PIN
  • Make sure the transaction is successful

What if the payment doesn't work?

Please try again later. It may have to do with the network connection or the connection to the card reader. Make sure the bluetooth of the tablet is turned on! If the payment still doesn't work, please ask your manager to contact Remy.

What are accepted payment methods?

All payments must be made by card. Accepted cards are Maestro, V-Pay, Mastercard, Visa.

The "Payment Links" option that you may encounter is not supported.
