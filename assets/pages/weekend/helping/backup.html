Backup

Backup shifts are unique in that they do not need to report at the information desk. Instead, you will be called for.

How do I know if I need to do something?

In case a backup shift is needed, you will be contacted through your team captain or called out by the presenter.

Where do I need to report?

Unless given a specific instruction, you will have to report to the information desk. There you will be instructed further.

What kind of tasks would I be doing?

This could be any of the other shifts as listed.

Can I do something else while it's my shift and I haven't been called for?

Yes, while you haven't been asked to report for your shift, you can do whatever you want as long as you are able to report at short notice. In case you leave the tournament hall, make sure your team captain or a team member knows where you are.
