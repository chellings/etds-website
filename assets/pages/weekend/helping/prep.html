Where do I report?

At the beginning of your shift you go to the info desk and will receive a task from the manager volunteers.

What are my tasks?

  • In general make sure the hall is cleaned and looks super good for the tournament or party.
  • Remove all trash bags and replace them with new ones.
  • Make sure everything is clean and put stuff that is left around the hall in the team boxes or at the lost & found at the info desk.
