Reception

Where do I report?

At the beginning of your shift you go to the reception tables and report to the manager reception; they will instruct you what to do.

What are my tasks?

As a receptionist, it is your job to welcome our lovely guests and prepare them for our event. The procedure for every participant is as follows:
  • Ask their name and the name of their team and check if they are on the list of participants.
    • If they are not, notify the manager reception.
  • If they are marked as a student, ask them to show you a student ID or proof of registration for the current academic year.
    • If they cannot show that they are a student, they have to pay a supplement to their entry fee. Ask the manager reception to help you with this.
  • If everything is correct, hand them their entry bracelet and mark them as registered.
  • If they ordered any merchandise, hand it to them and mark it on the merchandise sheet.
  • If they are the first team captain from their team to register, hand them a TC flyer and the numbers for their team.

What are important things to keep in mind?

  • The reception will be set up with three queues based on the names of the teams
  • People that are not participating in the tournament (Super‐volunteers/visitors/other helpers) don’t have to wait in line at the registration desk and can go to the tournament hall badge checkers directly. They will receive their badge there.

Someone needs to pay something, what do I do?

All payments are done by card. Cash is not accepted. To find out how to process payments check the pin machine help page for more information.

I have a question!

If you need anything or find yourself in a situation where you don’t know what to do: contact the manager reception.

At the end of your shift

Say goodbye to the manager reception, inform them of anything that they should know and release yourself from your shift.
