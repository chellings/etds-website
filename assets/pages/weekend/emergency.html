First aid schedule
Internal emergency plan

In case of emergency, the most important steps are:


Let your team captain know

Let the organization know (ideally through the team captain)

If nether of the above are possible: let the crew of the venue know.

In case of medical emergencies, there is a medical professional on duty 24/7 at the venue.

In case of fire or other emergencies, press the fire alarm button or call 112.

Make sure to relay the location of the event: X TU Delft, Mekelweg 8, 2628 CD Delft.
