Visitor information

Visitors are welcomed to the ETDS throughout the weekend. So be sure to invite your family and friends to come and watch you!

Information

Visitors are welcomed at the ETDS at any moment. This means you can have your family come and watch for the competition. You can also have someone from your association join for one of the parties to give them a taste of the ETDS!

There are two types of visitors:
Daytime visitors & Nighttime visitors.

Daytime visitors

Day visitors are welcome during the tournament to come and cheer on their friends and family. Daytime is from 08:00 until the end of the tournament, usually around 18:30.

Nighttime visitors

Night visitors are welcome during the evening parties for dancing and watching all shows. Nighttime is from the start of the evening program until the early morning. (For the exact times per day, see the program)

Signup

Daytime visitors can simply show up at the venue entrance. Nighttime visitors are required to sign up using this form. Without sign-up, entrance to the venue will not be possible. No exceptions will be made to this rule.

Payment

Entrance for daytime visitors is €5.
Entrance for nighttime visitors is €7,50.
This means that admission for an entire day is €12,50.

Food, Drinks

No food or drinks are included in the admission fee. However, drinks can be bought in the tournament hall and at the café. Food can also be purchased at the café until 21:00 and snacks until 23:00.

Ordering food to the venue is strictly forbidden.
