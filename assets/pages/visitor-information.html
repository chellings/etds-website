Limited number of daytime visitor tickets available

Information

Because of space limitations we have a limited number of daytime visitor (spectator) tickets available. If you or some of your friends or relatives are interested in coming to watch the tournament, please fill out the registration form.

Spectators can enter the tournament hall from 7:30 in the morning and are expected to leave the tournament hall until 30 minutes after the last final round of the tournament (scheduled at 18:30). Please note that this means that spectators will not be able to join the evening program, which includes the awards ceremonies.

We are currently not planning to offer evening (party) tickets. A daytime ticket does not include access to the evening program.
