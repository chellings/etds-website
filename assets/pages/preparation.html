Plates and cutlery

Our venue is eco-friendly, and we do not provide disposable items. Kindly bring your own cutlery, a plate, a cup, and a bowl for soup. If you forget something, we offer reusable merchandised cutlery. We have an industrial dishwasher available, and it will be used team-wise. A timetable will be provided on the tables for scheduling dishwasher use. Please note that this service is team/table-specific, ensuring efficient use.

No-Alcohol & Non-smoking campus

Alcohol and smoking are prohibited on all school premises. This includes the tournament hall, the sleeping halls and the entire school grounds including the whole parking area.

No deodorant in the halls

Smoke detectors are fitted in all sports halls which respond to deodorants and sprays. Please do not use spray deodorant in the halls!!!! (Neither in the sleeping halls nor in the tournament hall!) We would like to have an event without fire brigade!

Tournament Desk Internship

We are offering an internship position for the Tournament Desk team. This is a unique opportunity for someone interested in learning about tournament operations and systems. If you are interested in this position, please let us know.
