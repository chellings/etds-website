Food & Drinks

It is extremely important that you get your nutrients and your fluids during this tournament. This page tells you all about where to get your food and drinks, and what will be available.

You can find the menus for both days on the menu page.

Food

Included meals

The food that will be provided is:
  • A snack on Friday
  • Breakfast and lunch on Saturday
  • Breakfast, lunch and dinner on Sunday
  • Breakfast on Monday

Brunch

Breakfast and lunch will be combined into a brunch. In other words, food wil be served continuously from 07:30 until about 14:30. Brunch will be a typical Dutch bread and condiment buffet. Early afternoon, a well-filled soup (vegan minestrone) will be added to the buffet.

Since the buffet is open continuously, you can also get food as often as you want. However, we have food estimates based on the number of people. Therefore, please be very careful to avoid food waste. If we notice large amounts of uneaten sandwiches on the table you may be reprimanded.

Dinner

Dinner will be served on Sunday. On Saturday, there will only be dinner supplied for the team captains. All other participants are invited to either:
  • Go into Delft city center and enjoy some amazing food in one of the many restaurants.
  • Order an affordable meal at the X Café at the venue: €8,- which you can order here. (Until 29/09 12:00)
  • Exit the venue and order pizza's to the campus. (Ordering to the venue is strictly forbidden)

Snacks

A snack (also vegan option) will be served on Friday evening. Other snacks, such as bitterballen, can be ordered from the X Café until 23:00.

Allergies and intolerances

Please find all allergy information on the allergies page.

Drinks

Coffee & Tea

All day coffee and tea will be available until we run out.

Bar

There will be a bar inside the tournament hall (open until 2AM) selling the following items:
  • (Sparkling) water
  • Pepsi and Pepsi Max
  • Sisi (orange soda)
  • 7Up
  • Beer
  • Sparkling wine
All drinks will be sold per bottle, except for beer which will be sold per glass. We are still looking into the viability of selling the drinks per glass as well.

Drinks can only be paid for by debit card (Maestro/VPay). Prices will be added once they are finalized. Please note that for these prices we are bound to the venue's prices, and we do not attempt to make a profit on the drinks.

Other drinks

Other drinks can be bought from the X Café until midnight. Such drinks include:
  • Cappuccino, Latte Macchiato, etc.
  • Wine
  • Craft beers
  • Sports drinks
Tap water is also freely available and of high quality.

Bringing your own drinks is strictly forbidden.
