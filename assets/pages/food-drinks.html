Food & Drinks

It is very important that you get your nutrients and your fluids during the tournament. This page tells you all about where to get your food and drinks, and what will be available.

You can find the menus for both days on the menu page.

Food

Included meals

The food that will be provided is:
  • A snack on Friday
  • Breakfast, lunch and dinner on Saturday
  • Breakfast, lunch and dinner on Sunday
  • Breakfast on Monday

Dinner

Dinner will be served on Saturday and Sunday

Snacks

A (vegan) snack will be served on Friday evening.

Drinks

Coffee & Tea

All day coffee and tea will be available until we run out.

Bar

There will be a bar inside the tournament hall selling the following items:
  • Water (still or sparkling): 1.50€
  • Juice (apple or orange): 2.50€
  • Cola (regular or light): 2.50€
  • Fanta: 2.50€
  • Sprite: 2.50€
  • Mezzo Mix: 2.50€
  • Apple Spritzer: 2.50€
All drinks will be sold per bottle (1L). We will not be able to sell alcoholic drinks.

Drinks can only be paid for by debit card (Maestro/VPay). Tap water is also freely available and of high quality.
